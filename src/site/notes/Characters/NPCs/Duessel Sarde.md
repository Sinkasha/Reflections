#NPCs  
# Duessel Sarde 
Revolutionary Leader AKA the "Shadebringer"

Duessel Sarde is possibly a descendent of a clan that worshipped [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Religion of Faltera#Nyarlathotep\|Nyarlathotep]]. 

In [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/History of Faltera/213 CE - The Leader of the Revolutionaries Succumbs to the Mirror\|213 CE]], [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous/The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness\|The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness]] is presented to him by two mysterious Odemian mages. Nyarlathotep attempts to possess him, leading to Duessel going berserk and causing the [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/History of Faltera/213 CE - The Falteran Massacre\|The Falteran Massacre]]. 

[[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/History of Faltera/214 CE - The Subjugation of Duessel Sarde\|214 CE - The Subjugation of Duessel Sarde]]: Duessel's corpse was found and confirmed dead. The people who killed him were never seen again. 

Three people teamed up to kill Duessel: [[Characters/PCs/Bloo\|Bloo]], originally known as Sorean; [[Characters/NPCs/Astrid von Novis\|Astrid von Novis]]; and a tall drow women.
