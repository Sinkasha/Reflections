#NPCs 
# Grimnir
Spoopy mage

A wickedly powerful mage who eliminates all those who cross him. One of the only travelers to [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Empire of Dagdra#Thalanrod\|Thalanrod, Dagdra]]. 

Theories abound that he might be behind the auras around the world and by extension the disappearances. He is [[Characters/NPCs/Theodore\|Theodore]]'s master.
