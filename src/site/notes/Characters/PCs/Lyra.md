Laura's Gambling Half-Elf Sorcerer

She loves gambling. Her father is [[Characters/NPCs/Horace Frostdir\|Horace Frostdir]]. The human side of her family were the Goldrunes, unrelated to the city of Goldrune in Dagdra. She is from Gongaga, [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Empire of Dagdra\|Dagdra]].
