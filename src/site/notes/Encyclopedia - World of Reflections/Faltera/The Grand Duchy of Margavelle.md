The Grand Duchy of Margavelle is a smaller nation in Faltera. This nation has comparatively less influence in Faltera when compared to [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Kingdom of Fusilla\|Fusilla]] and [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Empire of Dagdra\|Dagdra]], but that is no reason to take this country lightly.
