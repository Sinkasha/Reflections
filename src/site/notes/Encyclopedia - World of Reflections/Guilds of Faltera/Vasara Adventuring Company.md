# Vasara Adventuring Company
Acting Chair: [[Characters/NPCs/Seliph Orsterra\|Seliph Orsterra]]

One of the two major Adventurer’s Guilds in Faltera, based in [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Kingdom of Fusilla#Vasara\|Vasara, Fusilla]]. The guild practices anti-discriminatory policies and is known for having a minimal cut in job postings. They offer quests for all skill levels and support fledgling and veteran adventurers alike.

It was founded by Roland Vasara, in honor of his elder brother [[Characters/PCs/Bloo\|Sorean]].
