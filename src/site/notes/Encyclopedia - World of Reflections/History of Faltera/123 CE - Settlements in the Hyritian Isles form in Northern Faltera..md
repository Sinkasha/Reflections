## 123 CE - Settlements in the Hyritian Isles form in Northern Faltera.
The largest island of [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Hyritian Alliance#Valzelia\|Valzelia]] has its first settlement, followed shortly thereafter by [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Hyritian Alliance#Dragale\|Dragale]] and then [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Hyritian Alliance#Wovram\|Wovram]]. Other settlements continue to form.
