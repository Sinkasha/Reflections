## 212 CE - The Hyritian Settlements form an Alliance
By lead of Valzelia, the peoples of the Hyritian Isles form a political and economic alliance, sensing a shift in the geopolitical climate of Faltera. ([[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Hyritian Alliance\|The Hyritian Alliance]])
