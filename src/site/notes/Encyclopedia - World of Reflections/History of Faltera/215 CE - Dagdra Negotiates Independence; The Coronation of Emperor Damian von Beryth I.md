## 215 CE - Dagdra Negotiates Independence; The Coronation of Emperor Damian von Beryth I
Thanks to the cooperation, [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Kingdom of Fusilla\|Fusilla]] is convinced to allow the revolutionaries their independence. Together they form a militant empire known as [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Empire of Dagdra\|Dagdra]]. Lord Damian von Beryth, hailed by the revolutionaries as a hero who leveraged independence without succumbing to the [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous/The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness\|Mirror]], was chosen by the people to lead. His coronation was a global event for all nations to see, marking a new era for Faltera.
