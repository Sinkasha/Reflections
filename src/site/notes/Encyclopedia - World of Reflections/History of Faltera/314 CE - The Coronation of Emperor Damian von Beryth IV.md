## 314 CE - The Coronation of Emperor Damian von Beryth IV 
Due to fears of another political disaster, this coronation was delayed by several months. The current emperor was crowned publicly in the capital city of [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Empire of Dagdra#Alvar\|Alvar]].
