## Silver Key with Fusillan Flag

Discovered [[Session Notes - Reflections/09 - 4.04.2024 - The Consequences of Our Actions#^06b005\|here]]. 
This key was entrusted to the [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Empire of Dagdra#Fiskov Family\|Fiskov family]], specifically [[Characters/NPCs/Fae Fiskov\|Fae Fiskov]]. 
It opens a vault in [[Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Faltera/The Kingdom of Fusilla#Parli\|Parli]] in the royal castle, which requires two keys. The other key is in Parli. 

According to [[Characters/NPCs/Horace Frostdir\|Horace Frostdir]] this key is a fake. 

It's currently in [[Characters/PCs/Nim\|Nim]]'s hands.
