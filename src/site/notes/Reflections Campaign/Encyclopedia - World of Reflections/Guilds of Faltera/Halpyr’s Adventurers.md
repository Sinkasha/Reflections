#guilds 
# Halpyr’s Adventurers
Leader: [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Melina Fiskov\|Melina Fiskov]] (long-time adventurer)

One of the two major Adventurer's Guilds in Faltera, based in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Empire of Dagdra#Halpyr\|Halpyr, Dagdra]]. This guild is known for having an internal structure that mirrors a meritocracy. Adventurers are given a rating at the end of each moon and their rank determines their influence.
