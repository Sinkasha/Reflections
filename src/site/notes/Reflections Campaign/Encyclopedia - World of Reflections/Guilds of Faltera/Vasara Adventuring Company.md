#dnd/reflections_campaign/guilds 
# Vasara Adventuring Company
Acting Chair: [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Seliph Orsterra\|Seliph Orsterra]]

One of the two major Adventurer’s Guilds in Faltera, based in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Kingdom of Fusilla#Vasara\|Vasara, Fusilla]]. The guild practices anti-discriminatory policies and is known for having a minimal cut in job postings. They offer quests for all skill levels and support fledgling and veteran adventurers alike.
