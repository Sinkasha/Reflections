## Silver Key with Fusillan Flag
*Colloquially referred to as the Count's balls* 

Discovered [[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/09 - 4.04.2024 - The Consequences of Our Actions#^06b005\|here]]. 
This key was entrusted to the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Empire of Dagdra#Fiskov Family\|Fiskov family]], specifically [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Fae Fiskov\|Fae Fiskov]]. 
It opens a vault in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Kingdom of Fusilla#Parli\|Parli]] in the royal castle, which requires two keys. The other key is in Parli. 

It's currently in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Nim\|Nim]]'s hands.
