## The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness
Created in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Holy State of Odema\|The Holy State of Odema]] in 212 CE by two mysterious mages. The mirror reveals to the beholder the worst traits in them, and magnifies it. This manifests in the beholder if they lack the resolve to fight off the temptation. If the beholder of the mirror can resist, they are granted total control over psychic and necrotic abilities. 

In 213 CE, the Revolutionary Leader, Duessel Sarde, succumbs to the temptation and goes berserk. What follows is the [[Reflections Campaign/Timeline - Reflections Campaign/213 CE - The Falteran Massacre\|Falteran Massacre]]. By 215 CE, this relic was destroyed and its shards separated.
