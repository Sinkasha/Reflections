#misc
## Sanctum Key

Discovered [[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/06 - 1.25.2024 - The Count's Balls#^f23f3c\|here]]. A copper plated key that's slightly rusted, related to [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous/The Fish Crystals\|The Fish Crystals]]. It has a vaguely fish-like pattern on it. 

It's currently in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Nim\|Nim]]'s hands. Used in [[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/14 - 5.23.2024 - FISH CRYSTALS\|14 - 5.23.2024 - FISH CRYSTALS]] to open a chest containing the last two [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous/The Fish Crystals\|fish crystals]].
