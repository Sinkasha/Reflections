#NPCs  
# Count Canonbury
The Dead Count

An elf who was once a student of the magic academy trying to become a legendary inventor. He attempted to  replicate the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous - Reflections Campaign/The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness\|The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness]] without the bad side effects. He worked as a scientist for [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/The Order of the Fish\|The Order of the Fish]]. 

There were rumors that he was either in possession of something the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Guilds of Faltera/The League of Larcenists\|The League of Larcenists]] wanted or was a a part of them ([[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/05 - 1.18.2024 - Canonbury Manor Heist#^040504\|here]]). In the same session, the party broke into his manor and attempted to steal his balls. 

He was suspicious of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Yul\|Yul]], [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Nim\|Nim]], and [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Bloo\|Bloo]] during their encounter ([[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/06 - 1.25.2024 - The Count's Balls#^2f57c6\|here]])

Count Canonbury was the lead research who had been researching people coming into the Material Plane from the Fae Wild through [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous - Reflections Campaign/The Count's Orb\|the orb]]. According to the fairies found at Count Canonbury's Manor, people have been entering the Fae Wild for decades ([[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/06 - 1.25.2024 - The Count's Balls#^a46d2e\|here]]). The Count was becoming more paranoid and distrustful as his research went on. 

The party successfully stole his balls. 

He mentions that he needs to meet up with the other bearers of the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous - Reflections Campaign/The Fish Crystals\|fish]] that was found in his bedroom. 

The count alleges that he is not affiliated with the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Guilds of Faltera/The League of Larcenists\|The League of Larcenists]] and instead, the LoL stole everything from the Count. The count is investigating the various portals that are popping up everywhere. 

He is not researching this with anyone else. 

[[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Bloo\|Bloo]] stabbed him. 

Seliph discovers that the Count had contacted both [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Astrid von Novis\|Astrid von Novis]] and her apprentice [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Horace Frostdir\|Horace Frostdir]] for funds. He failed magic school, the University of Southern Canonbury. He pretended to be a professional warmage but it fell apart when it was figured out he was using an eldritch cannon. 

As confirmed by his adopted son [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Jeice\|Jeice]], the League stole from the Count.
