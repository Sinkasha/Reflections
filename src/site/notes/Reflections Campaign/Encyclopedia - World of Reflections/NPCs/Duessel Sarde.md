#NPCs  
# Duessel Sarde 
Revolutionary Leader AKA the "Shadebringer"

Duessel Sarde is possibly a descendent of a clan that worshipped [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Religion of Faltera#Nyarlathotep\|Nyarlathotep]]. 

In [[Reflections Campaign/Timeline - Reflections Campaign/213 CE - The Leader of the Revolutionaries Succumbs to the Mirror\|213 CE]], [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous - Reflections Campaign/The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness\|The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness]] is presented to him by two mysterious Odemian mages. Nyarlathotep attempts to possess him, leading to Duessel going berserk and causing the [[Reflections Campaign/Timeline - Reflections Campaign/213 CE - The Falteran Massacre\|The Falteran Massacre]]. 

Duessel's corpse was found and confirmed dead. The people who killed him were never seen again.
