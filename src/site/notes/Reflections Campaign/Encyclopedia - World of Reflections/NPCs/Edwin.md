#NPCs 
# Edwin
*Samurai Duck*

A samurai duck, met during [[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/03 - 1.13.2023 - The Portal\|03 - 1.13.2023 - The Portal]] after the party went through a portal and then [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Bloo\|Bloo]] broke the dimension within and was promptly stabbed by Edwin. He was later named Edwin by [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Quill\|Quill]] and joined the party with Quill. 

In [[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/04 - 2.17.2023 - Personal Trials\|04 - 2.17.2023 - Personal Trials]] he slices open another domain and gives all the party members a personal trial, resulting in a reward of a fancy weapon. 

Was reunited [[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/16 - 06.07.2024 - The Big Boss#^fb3820\|here]]. Edwin knows that [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Bloo\|Bloo]] is Sorean and is currently the bodyguard to Queen Primrose L’Dore of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Kingdom of Fusilla\|The Kingdom of Fusilla]].
