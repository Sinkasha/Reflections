#NPCs  
# Ellery Woddlespin
Famous Weaponsmith

A dwarf. Made the weapons that killed [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Duessel Sarde\|Duessel Sarde]] during [[Reflections Campaign/History of Faltera/214 CE - The Subjugation of Duessel Sarde\|214 CE - The Subjugation of Duessel Sarde]]: a tome called Stasis, a sword called Dynamo, and a bow called Horizon. Stasis and Dynamo are currently in the possession of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Astrid von Novis\|Astrid von Novis]]

Also made the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous - Reflections Campaign/Space-Time Swords\|Space-Time Swords]]. 
Forged [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous - Reflections Campaign/Spellcleaver\|Spellcleaver]], which was stolen from Ellery
