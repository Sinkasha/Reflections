#NPCs 
# The Order of the Fish

The Order is a political faction based in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Empire of Dagdra#Windrest\|Windrest]]. Their goal is to overthrow the Queen of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Kingdom of Fusilla\|Fusilla]]. 

[[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Hugo Starling\|Hugo Starling]] believes that the Order is a purely political operation that hires the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Guilds of Faltera/The League of Larcenists\|The League of Larcenists]] *or* the League is a front for a particular wing of the Order. The Order blames Fusilla for the [[Reflections Campaign/Timeline - Reflections Campaign/313 CE - The Assassination of the Dagdran Emperor Damian von Beryth III\|assassination of Emperor Beryth III]], however, it was actually the Order who assassinated the Emperor as an inside job. The Order used this as an excuse after [[Reflections Campaign/Timeline - Reflections Campaign/214 CE - The Subjugation of Duessel Sarde\|Duessel was subjugated]] and [[Reflections Campaign/Timeline - Reflections Campaign/215 CE - Dagdra Negotiates Independence; The Coronation of Emperor Damian von Beryth I\|Dagdra became independent]] to achieve their goal of Dagdra conquering the rest of the continent. 

Related to [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous - Reflections Campaign/The Fish Crystals\|The Fish Crystals]]. Not all members of the Order follow [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Religion of Faltera#God of Death Nyarlathotep\|Nyarlathotep]], and the Nyarlathotep followers are using the Order.  Nyarlathotep followers' and the Order's goals sort of align. [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Guilds of Faltera/The League of Larcenists\|The League of Larcenists]] is a nonlethal branch of the Order. They are a distraction. 

The upper ranks of the Order all have markings on their face.
