#NPCs  
# Theodore
Unknown Member of the [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/The Order of the Fish\|The Order of the Fish]]

Apparently [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Mikhail\|Mikhail]] takes orders from him. He told Mikhail to "retrieve all remaining crystals in the pedestal, the ritual should not be initiated until further notice". 

Is a disciple of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Grimnir\|Grimnir]]. Was killed [[Reflections Campaign/Session Notes - Reflections/16 - 06.07.2024 - The Big Boss#^9e06d4\|here]] supposedly, but turns out it was a puppet similar to [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/PCs/Yul\|Yul]] and it also contained a shard of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/Miscellaneous/The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness\|The Mirror of Enveloping Darkness]]. 

His head is currently in Yul's possession
