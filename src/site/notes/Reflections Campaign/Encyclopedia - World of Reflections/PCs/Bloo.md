# Bloo
Sherri's Blob Sorcerer

Bloo woke up near the Geimshall Woods in [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Kingdom of Fusilla\|Fusilla]]. After the repeated mentioning of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/NPCs/Duessel Sarde\|Duessel Sarde]], Bloo feels a strong familiarity towards him, but a negative one. Bloo gets the feeling that they have met Duessel before they became Bloo. 

Using Detect Magic, one can sense multiple types of magic, including illusion and necromancy magic, concentrated around its head.
