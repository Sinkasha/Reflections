## 123 CE - Settlements in the Hyritian Isles form in Northern Faltera.
The largest island of [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Hyritian Alliance#Valzelia\|Valzelia]] has its first settlement, followed shortly thereafter by [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Hyritian Alliance#Dragale\|Dragale]] and then [[Reflections Campaign/Encyclopedia - World of Reflections/The Hyritian Alliance#Wovram\|Wovram]]. Other settlements continue to form.
